Services

The Plancksoft internet based startup provides many inductive and deductive services to its clients, including Software Engineering, Software Re-Engineering, and the Reverse Engineering of cross-platform Computing Systems based on the platforms of your choice.

Software Engineering

The systematic method of inducting a new cross-platform software system, or improving a pre-existing one based on the platforms of your choice.

Software Re-Engineering

The systematic deductive art of sherlocking a current system; which includes rebuilding all of its desired architectural blueprints and documentation.

Reverse Engineering & Systems Security

To probe an existing software system and figure out its security holes and patching them, or finding inefficiencies at a low-level/managed-level and optimizing them.

Software Testing

To blackbox test your software systems from the perspective of end-users from different backgrounds. Alternatively, whitebox testing your software systems from the perspective of battle-hardened veteran developers.

Consultation

To advise and present suitable technical solutions tailored to your current technical problems and hyper-charge your business.

Education, R&D

Invasion Zero

A free-to-play online multi-player first/third person shooter game

Invasion Zero is a multi-player online free to play first-shooter game supporting up-to 8 players per single session where you are able to play against enemy AI bots or players from all over the world. As the main player, you are equipped with a machine gun, shotgun, or sniper rifle depending on your class with a gun and a knife and a hand grenade. Your main objective is to survive the battle as much as possible while destroying the spawning enemy forces.

The game contains several playable modes such as:
1- Team Deathmatch (TDM)
2- Free-for-all (FFA)
3- Capture The Flag (CTF)
4- Gun Race (GR)
5- Domination / Cover Point (CP)
6- Search and Destroy (SND)
7- Elimination
8- Demolition

The game contains violence through ranged and melee weaponry piercing through the body of the enemy players.

  • Website: Invasion Zero
  • Available on: Steam, Google Play Store, Apple App Store
  • Developer: Plancksoft
  • Publisher: NeatVibez
  • Category: Action, Shooter
